Introduction

We're stripping away excess clutter from our landscapes, and reminding ourselves that pared back imagery with a minimal number of elements can be just as impactful and successful as when the frame is packed with detail. Nature is wonderful enough when viewed at the human scale. This photo gallery can maybe help serve as a reminder of the diversity of life on our planet, and that we must all do more to align our lives with the needs of the natural world if we're to give these species a chance of being existed.

Restriction

Before your submission, your work has to be:

  • Landscapes can include water, land and forest, but must also be wild
  • Human habitation and buildings can not be present in the landscaping shot
  • Landmarks can include buildings, architectures, and construction.
  • Human construction has to be in the landmark shot
  • Tourism shot can not be used in the landmark shot due to portraiture right
  • Landmark has to be easily indentified
  • Wild animals are required in Wildlife shot
  • Pets and zoo animals can not be present in the wildlife shot
  • All works have to be participants' original design
  • Participant will be disqualified once plagiarism is found

How it works

Every photo submitted will be available for the crowd to vote once the submissions period has ended. You can see all the images uploaded to a contest, but will need to rate them to see how they're ranked once the rating period begins. Some contests on Photocrowd also have a judge. After the submission period closes the judge chooses their favourite images and writes some image reviews. The crowd and judge results will be announced on the same day.

Who can apply

  • All age ranges
  • Below age 14 years old must leave a guardian's contact
  • Professionals, Ametuers, and volunteers

Image Requirement

  • No demensions restriction
  • Best to keep size under 1 Mb
  • Image typescan include .jpg, .jpeg, .png
  • No .gif or other web type of images allowed
