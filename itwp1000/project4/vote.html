Voting System

We are looking for photos that show author are dedicated to bettering craft, excited about technical and creative excellence, and ready to keep moving forward toward their unique goals.

Vote for your favorite photos and help us find the winner of this photo contest. The more you vote the better the results. The top photos receiving the most votes from our audience will be viewed by our judges. One winner out of the most voted photos will be selected as the People's Choice Winner.

There are three categories, so make sure you vote one from each categories. If you also want to be in this contest, go to Submission page and let us see your work! Learn more about Rules and prepare for next contest.
